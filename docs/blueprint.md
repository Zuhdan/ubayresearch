# **App Name**: Ubay Research Hub

## Core Features:

- Research Publication Display: Showcase research papers, articles, and reports with detailed information.
- Researcher Profiles: Dedicated pages for each of the 3 members, highlighting their expertise, publications, and contact information.
- AI-Powered Research Summaries: Provide concise summaries of research papers using a generative AI tool.
- Contact Form: Allow visitors to send messages to the researchers

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1E3A8A), conveying trust, stability, and knowledge.
- Background color: Off-white (#F9FAFB), providing a clean, professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted teal (#34D399) for interactive elements and highlights, suggesting innovation.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif for a modern, neutral look.
- Use minimalist icons to represent research areas and tools.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout for easy navigation and readability.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
